## Color Scheme (Material 3)

### Light Mode:
- **Primary:** Deep Purple (#673AB7)
- **Active Status:** Green (#4CAF50)
- **Idle Status:** Orange (#FF9800)
- **Completed Status:** Gray (#9E9E9E)
- **User Messages:** Blue Container
- **Cursor Messages:** Purple Container
- **Background:** Light Gray/White

### Dark Mode:
- Same color seeds
- Automatically adapts contrast
- Dark surface colors
- Elevated components

## Interactive Elements

### Buttons:
- **FAB:** Rounded, primary color, with icon + text
- **Send:** Icon button, primary color
- **Refresh:** Icon button in app bar
- **Info:** Icon button in app bar

### Cards:
- **Elevation:** Slight shadow
- **Ripple Effect:** On tap
- **Border Radius:** 12px
- **Padding:** 12px inside

### Input:
- **Border:** Rounded (24px radius)
- **Multi-line:** Expands as needed
- **Hint Text:** Context-aware
